About me

I like to work on a variety of things! Most of them are listed on the projects page, but I always find myself messing around with new things!

I'm a huge fan of taking the time to learn how things work under the hood, and trying not to rely on other things abstracting ideas away! It's why I chose to build this site in raw HTML/CSS, despite a lot of experience with React in large codebases. Its also a reason I host my source code on Codeberg instead of Github now, and instead focus on having robust personal backups for data I care about. It's a fun project!

I love my little side-hobbies too! I'm really enjoy pole dancing in particular, so if you ever want a conversation starter with me that's an easy go-to. Otherwise, I'm a huge fan of music and try to keep my tastes pretty diverse, so I love talking about all sorts of genres and styles.

If you ever want to contact me, you can always reach me on Mastodon here. Otherwise, feel free to email me at me [at] callumjones.dev.
